Car Tracking & Identification

Working from the ground of the basic building stone for the project. We detect every vehicle entering the parking lot. There are some many vehicles entering a parking lot everyday. To ensure the safety and constant eye on vehicles, we enable Car tracking inside the parking lot to avoid any mishappening or any miscellaneous activity inside the parking lot. Vehicle tracking systems are also popular in consumer vehicles as a theft prevention, monitoring and retrieval device. Police can simply follow the signal emitted by the tracking system and locate the stolen vehicle. When used as a security system, a Vehicle Tracking System may serve as either an addition to or replacement for a traditional car alarm. Our system keeps a check on your vehicle using CCTVs and assist the security agencies to keep a close look on vehicles either entering or moving inside a parking lot.
